Recipe #1

  • Time: 1 hour 30 min;
  • Serves: 15 min

Ingredients:

Ingredients Quantity
Meat 1000 gramm
Butter 100 gramm
Salt 25 gramm

Steps:

  1. Step 1: Put butter on the frying pan;

    • Cut the butter into a square;
    • Fry the pan;
    • Put butter on top of the frying pan.

  2. Step 2: Put meat on the frying pan;

    • Cut the meat an oval shape;
    • Salt it on the front and on the back;

  3. Step 3: Salt the meat.

    • A little of seasoning on the sause in the pan.
